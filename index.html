About Us ▲

Established in 1964 by K Azeez Ahmed, Jai Hind Tailoring Hall has been serving generations with quality craftsmanship and perfect fitting.

Now led by Mohammed Ali, we continue the legacy of excellence. We specialize in designer blouse stitching, bridal embroidery work, custom neck patterns, and precision alterations.

Our commitment to detail, finishing, and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted name in Chikkaballapur for over 60 years.
